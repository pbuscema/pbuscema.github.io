Hilma af Klint

Primordial Chaos (1906-1907)

Group I, Primordial Chaos, 1906-1907

  • First group in the series depicting the birth of the world and the dualities af Klint believed to be a principle part of life
  • Consists of a progression across 26 canvases
  • Colors representative of gender roles: yellow is male, blue is female, green is unity of the genders

Pamela Buscema & Laiba Sarwar
